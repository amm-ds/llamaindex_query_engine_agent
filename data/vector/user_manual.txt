## 2. Getting Started
To set up your NeoGadget X1:
1. Press and hold the power button for 3 seconds.
2. Follow the on-screen instructions to connect to Wi-Fi.
3. Log in with your NeoAccount or create a new one.
4. Allow the device to download and install the latest updates.

## 3. Features
### 3.1 NeoVoice Assistant
Activate NeoVoice by saying "Hey Neo" followed by your command.

### 3.2 NeoProjekt
Project your screen onto any flat surface by double-tapping the top of the device.

### 3.3 NeoCharge
Place your NeoGadget X1 on any Qi-compatible wireless charging pad for hassle-free charging.

## 4. Troubleshooting
### 4.1 Device Won't Turn On
1. Ensure the battery is charged.
2. Try a force restart by holding the power and volume down buttons for 10 seconds.

### 4.2 NeoVoice Not Responding
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Ensure the microphone is not obstructed.
3. Update to the latest firmware version.
